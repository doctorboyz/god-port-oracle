# Easy Box (EZB) + 1CB Trading Philosophy

## Core Philosophy

**"ท่าง่าย ทำซ้ำได้ วัดผลได้"** — Simple setup, repeatable, measurable.

- Trading is a game of statistics and probability, not guessing
- WR 40-50% is sufficient if risk management is solid
- A simple trade setup that you can repeat consistently beats 10 complex setups you can't
- No holy grail exists — success comes from discipline + simplicity + risk management

## Key Concepts Applicable to God Port

### 1. Risk Per Trade (RPT) — Already aligned
- 1-3% risk per trade, always use SL
- SL is your friend, not your enemy — 1 losing trade without SL can wipe 99 wins
- Position size = risk % × capital / SL distance

### 2. Multi-Timeframe Top Down — Maps to our MTF system
| TF Level | Purpose | Tool |
|----------|---------|------|
| Large (Day/H4) | Trend direction | Price structure (HH/HL, LH/LL) + Close Level + DWZ/SWZ |
| Mid (H1) | Zone identification | Close Level + DWZ/SWZ overlap = strongest level |
| Small (M5-M30) | Entry timing | Rejection in zone + confirmation candle |

- All 3 layers must agree before entry
- If any layer conflicts, skip the trade
- Author uses M20 for entry (personal preference, not universal)

### 3. Four Structure Types for S/R — Applicable to signal generation
1. **Close Level** — Close price > Wick. Close = where the winning side held control at session end
2. **Demand Wick Zone (DWZ)** — Lower wick zone where buying pressure reversed price
3. **Supply Wick Zone (SWZ)** — Upper wick zone where selling pressure reversed price
4. **Flip Zone** — Broken support becomes resistance, broken resistance becomes support

**Implication for broky**: Our signal generator should weight Close Level interactions higher than Wick-level touches.

### 4. Six Candlestick Patterns Only — Simplifies signal validation
**Price Action (4)**:
- Bullish Engulfing
- Bearish Engulfing
- Piercing Line
- Dark Cloud Cover

**Price Pattern (2)**:
- Morning Star
- Evening Star

**Critical rule**: These patterns are ONLY valid at key structural levels (Close Level, DWZ, SWZ, Trend Line). Patterns in empty space = skip.

**Implication for broky**: Our confidence scoring should boost signals that form at structural levels and penalize signals in empty space.

### 5. Universal Entry Rules — Simplifies execution
1. Wait for confirmation candle to CLOSE (never use wick)
2. Enter at 50% retracement of confirmation candle body
3. SL behind confirmation candle (max 300 pips for XAUUSD)
4. TP at next Close Level, DWZ/SWZ, or RR 1:2+

**Implication for metty**: Entry rules can be codified — 50% retrace + SL behind candle + TP at structure.

### 6. EZB System (5 Steps) — Structured market reading
1. **Channel** — Draw channel to identify direction
2. **Easy Box** — Box the consolidation range
3. **Breakout with 1CB** — Wait for 1CB to confirm breakout direction
4. **Retest** — Price returns to box edge, confirms support/resistance flip
5. **Trade Plan** — Entry, SL, TP all defined before entry

### 7. 1CB (1 Candle Box) — Single candle confirmation
**Two contexts**:
- **Trend context**: 1CB at key zone (DWZ/SWZ/Close Level) confirms trend continuation
- **Range context**: 1CB + EZB confirms breakout from consolidation

**6 Iron Rules of 1CB**:
1. Always wait for candle close — never trade on wick
2. Never chase after candle closes — wait for natural pullback
3. Wait for pullback/rebound naturally
4. Enter at 50% of 1CB body (Open to Close)
5. SL behind 1CB, max 300 pips
6. TP at next DWZ/SWZ/Close Level, or RR 1:2+

### 8. Trading Psychology (10 Points)
1. You can't win every trade — accept it, use SL always
2. WR and RRR are inversely related — find your balance (author chose WR 40-50%)
3. Always have a trading plan
4. Journal every trade — not to remember, but to learn from mistakes
5. Review and improve regularly — markets change, systems must adapt
6. Don't compare profits with others — run your own race
7. Discipline > win/loss ratio — follow the plan, statistics do the work
8. Don't chase fancy strategies or trade on tips
9. Never risk too much — 1% per trade is safe, you need chance #2, #3, #4
10. The end goal is freedom, money is the tool — "เราไม่ได้อยู่เพื่อเทรด เราเทรดเพื่ออยู่"

## EZB+1CB System Weakness
- Requires HIGH patience — some days/weeks have zero signals
- 3-5 trades per week, not 30 per day
- Not suitable for traders who need constant action
- This patience requirement is actually a feature, not a bug — it filters out low-quality setups
