# Phase 1.5 Learnings

## ATR Multiplier Is the Biggest WR Lever
Changing ATR multiplier from 1.5 → 2.0 improved WR by +5pp (48% → 53%).
Wider SL = fewer stop-outs = higher WR. This was more impactful than any indicator change.

## Session Filtering Helps but Has Limits
Asian=0.70 + Overlap=1.10 added +2pp WR. Further reduction (0.50, 0.00) actually hurt WR because it filtered good trades too.

## MTF Hard Filter = Same as Soft Filter
Halving confidence for counter-trend signals pushes them below min_confidence anyway.
The practical effect of soft (*0.5) and hard (block) MTF filters is identical.

## Bollinger/ADX Adjustment: Minimal Impact
ADX 20-25 zone trades are rare because most qualifying signals have ADX ≥ 25.
Changing Bollinger from ±1.0 to ±0.3 in this zone had negligible effect.

## Volatile Regime Confidence Reduction Hurts PF
Reducing confidence in volatile regime filtered out profitable trades along with bad ones.
PF dropped from 1.50 to 1.45. Better to just label regime without penalizing confidence.

## WR Has a Ceiling with Current Indicators
Maximum achievable WR ≈ 53% on 2024-2026 data. WR ≥ 55% requires new indicators (RSI divergence, volume profile, order flow).

## Forward Test Engine Built
broky/forward/engine.py — paper trading engine with weekly reports.
CLI: `broky forward --paper --start 2025-12-01`

## Core Philosophy: Proxy Metrics ≠ Real Goal
PF=1.64 + MaxDD=11.8% IS profitable and safe, even at WR=53%.
The real goal: port doesn't blow up + grows consistently.
When proxy metrics conflict with the real goal, the real goal wins.
